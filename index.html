Services

WHERE WE WORK
We offer professional property management services and great rental options
throughout Calgary, Alberta, and surrounding areas. If you are a second-home
owner, a serious investor or anywhere in between, we provide customized
management solutions designed to help you get the most out of your investment.

HOW WE WORK
We understand what it takes to develop and maintain a successful rental property.
We can take care of every detail required to keep your property in great condition
as well as attract and retain quality renters. Our services include marketing
vacancies, screening renters, routine maintenance coordination, late-night phone
calls, accounting, legal compliance and more. You can be as involved as you like
or just leave it all to us

WE SPECIALIZE

We specialize in the management of multi-family properties as well as
single-family homes. If you own residential rentals in Calgary or the
surrounding area, please contact us to discuss your management needs
